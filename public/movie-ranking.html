The Best Movies According to Yash

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception

A mind-bending, visually stunning masterpiece that keeps you guessing until the very end.

The Shawshank Redemption

A poignant and gripping tale of friendship, hope, and redemption that will leave you deeply moved.

The Dark Knight

A dark and thrilling superhero film that transcends the genre, thanks to a mesmerizing performance by Heath Ledger as the Joker.
